Refried Beans with Corn Tortillas

Description

Refried beans garnished with queso fresco is a perfect side dish for any Mexican breakfast, lunch, or dinner. Incorporate corn tortillas to get creative with this dish.

Ingredients

  • 2 cans pinto beans
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1/2 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder

Steps

  1. Heat up a stainless steel saucepan to medium heat and add olive oil.
  2. Once heated, pour in the canned pinto beans. Add the seasoning powders plus salt and pepper for flavor.
  3. Here you can optionally add water depending on how thick you want the refried beans. Turn up the heat a bit until a simmer and then turn off the heat.
  4. Mash the beans and garnish with queso fresco. Serve with corn tortillas.
