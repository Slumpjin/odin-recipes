Mexican Coca-Cola

Description

A Mexican Coca-Cola goes amazing with a hearty breakfast. It uses cane sugar instead of high fructose corn syrup as it's sweetener. Make sure to enjoy it in moderation!

Ingredients

  • 1 glass bottle of Mexican Coca-Cola

Ingredients

  1. Chill the Mexican Coca-Cola in the freezer for 15 minutes.
  2. Enjoy!
