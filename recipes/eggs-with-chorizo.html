Eggs with Chorizo

Description

Eggs with chorizo is a staple breakfast for the people of Mexico. It is quick and simple to make so it is perfect for a satisfying breakfast.

Ingredients

  • 6-8 eggs
  • 1 chorizo sausage
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1/4 diced white onion

Steps

  1. Start by heating a stainless steel/cast iron pan to medium heat. Add your olive oil.
  2. Once heated, throw in the diced onions until nearly carmelized.
  3. Toss the entire chorizo sausage and break it up in the pan. Make sure the chorizo is almost entirely brown.
  4. Finally, crack your eggs into the pan and fully cook. You can optionally scramble your eggs if you like.
  5. You shouldn't worry about seasoning since the chorizo has plenty of spices but you can add salt and pepper to enhance the flavor.
